Introduction

Spencer the Spectrometer was a project created by Lauren Ames and Katie Le for a science project based on making a spectacle light or sound wave display.
The spectrometer was made to describe the effect different light waves had and what that light tells people of the contents of the source using digital graphs.

Feedback

The only feedback given by our science teacher was to improve the interactivity and connection between the audience and science in a fun way. The project had the amazing science and evidence to explain the inner workings of light and what it means, but in future could be more interactive to make it more inclusive for everyone to enjoy
